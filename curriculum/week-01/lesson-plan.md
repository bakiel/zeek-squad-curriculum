# Week 1: Welcome to Zeek Squad

## Lesson Overview

**Theme:** Introduction to Zeek Squad
**Duration:** 5 sessions (45 minutes each)
**Age Group:** 6-12 years

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, learners will be able to:
- Explain what Zeek Squad is about
- Identify the Zeek Squad characters and their traits
- Demonstrate classroom routines and expectations
- Work cooperatively with classmates

## Materials Needed

- Zeek Squad character posters
- Name tags/badges
- Zeek Squad welcome booklet
- Coloring sheets
- Team building game materials

---

## Day 1: Welcome Session

### Opening (10 min)
- Welcome song/chant
- Introduce yourself as the Zeek Squad leader
- Show the Zeek Squad logo and explain the mission

### Main Activity (25 min)
- **Meet the Characters**
  - Zeek the Robot (problem solver)
  - Byte the Bug (debugging expert)
  - Loop the Cat (pattern finder)
  - Pixel the Artist (creative coder)
- Character matching activity
- Design your own Zeek Squad badge

### Closing (10 min)
- Review characters
- Preview tomorrow's adventure
- Exit ticket: "I'm excited to learn about..."

---

## Day 2: Our Zeek Squad Rules

### Opening (10 min)
- Zeek Squad greeting
- Recap characters

### Main Activity (25 min)
- **Creating Our Code of Conduct**
  - Respect (listen when others speak)
  - Responsibility (take care of equipment)
  - Resilience (keep trying when it's hard)
  - Ready (come prepared to learn)
- Create classroom rules poster together
- Role-play scenarios

### Closing (10 min)
- Rules review game
- Zeek Squad pledge

---

## Day 3: Getting to Know Each Other

### Opening (10 min)
- Zeek Squad warm-up
- Review rules

### Main Activity (25 min)
- **Team Building Games**
  - Human Bingo (find someone who...)
  - Two Truths and a Wish
  - Collaborative puzzle challenge
- Form Zeek Squad teams (4-5 learners each)
- Create team names and cheers

### Closing (10 min)
- Team presentations
- High-five circle

---

## Day 4: What Will We Learn?

### Opening (10 min)
- Team check-in
- Zeek Squad chant

### Main Activity (25 min)
- **Programme Preview**
  - Watch intro video (if available)
  - Flip through workbook together
  - Highlight exciting activities coming up
- **Goal Setting**
  - "By the end of Zeek Squad, I want to..."
  - Decorate goal cards

### Closing (10 min)
- Share goals with partner
- Start portfolio folders

---

## Day 5: Celebration Friday

### Opening (10 min)
- Week review
- Celebrate small wins

### Main Activity (25 min)
- **Fun Activities**
  - Zeek Squad colouring pages
  - Character dress-up relay
  - Dance party with coding-themed songs
- Photo opportunity with Zeek Squad backdrop

### Closing (10 min)
- Weekly reflection
- Preview Week 2
- Certificates for completing Week 1

---

## Assessment

### Observation Checklist
- [ ] Participates in activities
- [ ] Shows respect for classmates
- [ ] Demonstrates enthusiasm for learning
- [ ] Works cooperatively in team

### Portfolio Items
- Zeek Squad badge
- Goal card
- Coloring page

## Differentiation

**Support:** Pair with buddy, simplified activities
**Extension:** Leadership roles, help others

## Home Connection

Send home:
- Welcome letter to parents
- Character introduction sheet
- "Ask me about Zeek Squad" prompt card
