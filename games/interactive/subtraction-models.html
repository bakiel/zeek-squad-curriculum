Pictures Show Subtraction!

A subtraction MODEL is a picture that shows taking away!

We draw items and cross out the ones we take away. The ones left are the answer!

Model for 5 - 2 = 3:

We see 5 apples. 2 have an X on them (taken away). 3 are left!

5 - 2 = 3

Model for 4 - 1 = 3:

4 stars total. 1 crossed out. 3 remain!

4 - 1 = 3
