What is Subtraction?

Subtraction means TAKING AWAY!

When we subtract, we start with a group and take some away. We count what is LEFT!

Example: We have 5 cubes:

Take away 2 cubes...

5 - 2 = 3

We have 3 cubes left!

Another example: 4 stars, take away 1:

4 - 1 = 3

The minus sign - means take away!

Start - Take Away = What is Left
