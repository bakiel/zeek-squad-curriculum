Meet the Letter BET!

בּ
Tap the letter to hear it!

The Dagesh: See that DOT inside? That is called a dagesh! When BET has a dagesh, it says /b/ like BAYIT (house)!

Step 1: Start at the top-right corner. Draw a line going LEFT across the top.

BET vs VET

בּ
BET
with dot = /b/
ב
VET
no dot = /v/

Remember: BET and VET look the same! The only difference is the dot (dagesh). BET has a dot = /b/ sound. VET has NO dot = /v/ sound.
