A subtraction sentence uses numbers and symbols!

We look at a picture and write: START - TAKE AWAY = LEFT

Picture: 5 fish, 2 swim away

5 - 2 = 3

5 is how many we start with. 2 is how many go away. 3 is how many are left!

Picture: 4 birds, 3 fly away

4 - 3 = 1

Look at the picture, fill in the sentence!
