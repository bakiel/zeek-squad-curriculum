Cube Trains Break Apart!

A cube train is a row of cubes connected together!

When we BREAK a cube train, we take some cubes away. That is subtraction!

5 cubes, break off 2:

5-2=3

The 3 cubes that stay are the answer!

4 cubes, break off 3:

4-3=1
Click cubes to break them off!
