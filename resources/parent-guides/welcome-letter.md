# Welcome to Zeek Squad!

Dear Parent/Guardian,

We are thrilled to welcome your child to **Zeek Squad**, our exciting after-school coding and digital literacy programme!

## What is Zeek Squad?

Zeek Squad is designed to introduce children to the world of coding and computational thinking through fun, engaging activities. Your child will learn valuable skills that prepare them for the digital future while having a great time!

## What Will My Child Learn?

Over the course of the programme, your child will:

- **Think Like a Coder**: Break down problems, find patterns, and create solutions
- **Understand Basic Coding Concepts**: Sequences, loops, conditionals, and more
- **Be a Good Digital Citizen**: Online safety, respect, and responsibility
- **Work as a Team**: Collaboration, communication, and creative problem-solving

## How Can I Support My Child?

1. **Ask Questions**: "What did you learn in Zeek Squad today?"
2. **Play Along**: Try the home activities together
3. **Celebrate Effort**: Praise persistence, not just success
4. **Be Patient**: Coding takes practice - mistakes are learning!
5. **Stay Connected**: Read the weekly newsletters
